## 🛠️ Module Summaries & Key Deliverables

### Module 1: Digital Literacy Awareness
Created a visual guide titled **"The Student Digital Framework"** using **Canva**. It focuses on three critical pillars: identifying credible information, maintaining a professional footprint, and adopting robust cybersecurity habits like using encrypted password managers[cite: 136].

### Module 2: Professional Digital Presence
Established a "living transcript" across three primary platforms:
* **GitHub:** For version-control and documenting my evolution as an engineer.
* **LinkedIn:** To connect with the VIT Bhopal community and build a reliable personal brand.
* **Kaggle:** My primary venue for immersive data science challenges and competitive programming.

### Module 3: Collaboration & Coding
* **Coding:** Successfully completed the **"Python If-Else"** challenge on HackerRank to demonstrate algorithmic logic.
* **Collaboration:** Developed a **"Cyber-Awareness Survey"** via Google Forms to test peer knowledge of phishing and 2FA, linked to a Google Sheet for automated data organization.

Researched the impact of email etiquette on career opportunities. Drafted formal communications for academic extension requests and professional internship inquiries to ensure interactions remain respectful and effective.

### Module 5: Cybersecurity Awareness
Investigated the **"Request Money" UPI Scam**, highlighting how social engineering subverts technical security. I have adopted a strict **"Pause before PIN"** rule to ensure I never enter a PIN for incoming transactions.
